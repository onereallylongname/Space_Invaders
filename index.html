Space Invaders


  • Arrows to move ← →
  • Spacebar to shoot

Shoot the invaders. A hit gives you points, and a miss makes you lose points.

Your luck is tied to your accuracy, try not to miss.

NOTE: Allowing the use of the microphone improves the experience (no sound is recorded, but the the sound intensity is monitored to make the stars blink).


Good luck!
