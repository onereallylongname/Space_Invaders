Space Invaders


  • Controls
  • Arrows to move ← →
  • Spacebar to shoot
  • R to restart
  • L turn on/off lazer
  • S turn on/off sound
  • F turn on/off frameRate
  • Drop name
  • Xs - Extra shot speed
  • Mt - Power multiplier
  • Cd - Cooldown
  • Pr - Pierce invaders
  • Hp - Ship HP (not in use)
  • Ss - Shot size
  • Drop color
  • Red        - harm skill
  • Yellow  - help a litle
  • Green    - are what you want

Shoot the invaders. A hit gives you points, and a miss makes you lose points.

Your luck is tied to your accuracy, try not to miss.

NOTE: Allowing the use of the microphone improves the experience (no sound is recorded, but the the sound intensity is monitored to make the stars blink).


Good luck!
